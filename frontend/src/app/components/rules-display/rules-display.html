7hand Rules

Objective: Be the first player to get rid of all cards in your hand by forming sets and runs.

Turn Structure

  1. Draw: Draw a card from the deck to your hand
  2. Qualify on the board: (Optional) Play a valid hand made of sets or runs from your hand to the table
  3. Play on the board: (Optional) Add cards to existing sets/runs on the table
  4. End Turn/Discard: Discard one card from your hand to the discard pile

Sets (3 or more cards of the same rank)

7♥
7♠
7♦

Runs (4 minimum consecutive cards of the same suit)

4♥
5♥
6♥
7♥

Special Rules

  • Aces can be low (A-2-3) or high (Q-K-A) but not both in the same run
  • You must have a qualified hand played on the board to play cards from your hand on to the board
  • The hand ends when a player has no cards left in their hand
  • Points are scored based on cards remaining in opponents' hands

Scoring

  • Joker: 15 points each
  • Number cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K, A): 10 points each

Qualified Hands

  • 1: (6 cards min) Two sets
  • 2: (7 cards min) One set and one run
  • 3: (8 cards min) Two runs
  • 4: (9 cards min) Three sets
  • 5: (10 cards min) Two sets and one run
  • 6: (11 cards min) One set and two runs
  • 7: (12 cards min) Three runs
