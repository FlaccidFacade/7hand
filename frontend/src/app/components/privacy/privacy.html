Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our app. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to provide personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior.

How Google AdSense Works

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our app and/or other sites on the Internet.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to enable more relevant advertising across the web
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings
  • Users may opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our app and store certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data that are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the app to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our app
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver personalized ads and measure ad performance
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our app.

Personalized Advertising

We may display personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. These ads are delivered through our advertising partners, including Google AdSense.

How Personalized Ads Work

Personalized advertising uses information about your activities across websites and apps to show you ads that are more relevant to your interests. This includes:

  • Websites you visit
  • Apps you use
  • Content you interact with
  • Your approximate location

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can control personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google's Ad Settings
  • Using the DAA's WebChoices Tool
  • Using the EDAA's opt-out page (for users in Europe)
  • Adjusting your device settings to limit ad tracking (iOS or Android)

Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will no longer see ads, but the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.
